Hide It version 1.0

INTRODUCTION

HideIt is a program which is used for hiding your confidential data from 'prying eyes'. The program very cleverly hides your data inside other files so that nobody even gets a hint of your private data. You can safely carry your data or keep the data in the hard disk without any problem. The program also has the option of extracting the hidden data from the target files. Password Protection ,Encryption and Invisible mode options are also available for increased security.
